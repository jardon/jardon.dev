In the AI World, Learning is Still Worth It

2025-12-16

My resume says that I am a software engineer. In practice, the many hats I’ve worn feel like an amalgomation of everything but. I have two bachelor’s degrees (dont ask), but long past were the days where I really felt like I excelled at my craft. Always writing some overly practical, never exceptional Python. Always frustrated with some dreadfully cobbled together Node application thats only ever just good enough. Now, I don’t think this is from a lack of ability or education. While no one would ever accuse me a star student, I did very much accel at data structures and alike, but somewhere along the way I traded pride for apathy.
